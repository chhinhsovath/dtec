## ğŸ“ PARTNER SCHOOLS & PRACTICUM

### **Partner Schools: 3-5 Schools (Recommended)**
**Rationale:**
- Small enough to manage
- Large enough for diverse experience
- Mix of school types (urban, semi-urban, rural)
- Different grade levels (Primary, Lower Secondary, Upper Secondary)

**Example Structure:**
```
Partner School 1: Urban Primary School (Grades 1-3)
Partner School 2: Urban Secondary School (Grades 7-9)
Partner School 3: Rural Primary School (Grades 1-6)
Partner School 4: Semi-urban School with diverse learners
Partner School 5: (Optional) International/Bilingual school
```

### **Practicum Duration & Hours**
- **Practicum Duration:** 10 weeks (Weeks 13-22)
- **Teaching Hours Target:** 120-150 teaching hours minimum
  - (About 12-15 hours per week for 10 weeks)
  - Equivalent to 3-4 full class periods per day
- **Observation Frequency:**
  - **Weekly 1:1 Observations** by mentor (1-2 per week)
  - **Bi-weekly Formal Evaluations** with feedback
  - **Monthly Portfolio Reviews** with coordinator

### **Supervision Structure**
- **Primary Supervisor:** Mentor (from university/training center)
  - Conducts observations
  - Provides feedback
  - Tracks competency progress
  - Submits assessment scores

- **Secondary Supervisor:** School Placement Supervisor (principal/senior teacher)
  - Provides daily support
  - Ensures school integration
  - Contributes to overall assessment
  - Signs off on completion
